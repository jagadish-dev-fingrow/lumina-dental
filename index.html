Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept my insurance?

We accept most major PPO insurance plans. Our front desk team will happily verify your benefits prior to your appointment to ensure you understand your coverage fully. We also offer our Lumina Membership for uninsured patients.

Is the laser cleaning really painless?

Yes! Our guided biofilm therapy uses a warm mix of air, water, and specialized powder to gently remove plaque without the scraping and sensitivity of traditional cleanings.

How long does teeth whitening take?

Our in-office advanced laser whitening takes approximately 45 minutes to an hour. You can expect your teeth to be up to 8 shades whiter by the time you leave our clinic.

What if I have an emergency after hours?

We have a 24/7 emergency line available for our patients. Simply call our main number, and follow the prompts to be connected to the on-call dentist.
